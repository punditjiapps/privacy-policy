Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Pundit Ji Online (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Pundit Ji Online and our website https://www.punditjionline.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: Email address, phone number, full name
  • Account Information: Username, password, and authentication details
  • Session Data: Booking history, session duration, astrologer interactions
  • Communication Data: In-app chat messages, voice/video consultation logs
  • Usage Data: Device information, crash logs, and performance analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize one-on-one astrology consultations
  • To manage session credits and booking functionality
  • To send updates and reminders related to sessions
  • To monitor app performance and improve our services

3. Credit System and In-App Purchases

Users may optionally purchase in-app credits to book astrology sessions. These credits have no monetary value outside the app and are solely used for booking personal consultations. All payments are securely processed via third-party providers (e.g., Razorpay).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade your data. We may share it with service providers (such as payment processors) solely for the purpose of delivering our services, or if required by law.

5. Data Security

We use secure technologies (HTTPS, TLS encryption) to safeguard your data in transit and storage.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary for service delivery. Inactive accounts and related data are automatically removed after 90 days of inactivity.

7. Your Rights

You can access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at punditjiapps@gmail.com.

8. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data directly from the app by navigating to the Profile section and selecting the “Delete Account” option. This will permanently remove your personal information, session history, and all associated data from our systems.

If you experience any issues, you can also request deletion by contacting us at punditjiapps@gmail.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
